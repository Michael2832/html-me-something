The Very Best Animal of them all!

The Western Painted Turtle

  • Enjoyable to watch

    The best turtle habitats are aquariums. These consist of the glass container, water, a filter, heat lamp, and floating dock.

  • Easy to maintain

    Once set up, the turtle aquarium needs minimal maintenance. If you keep the filters clean and the water levels filled, you will have a happy turtle. You can even get an auto timer to have the light turn on and off at set times. And with the addition of an auto feeder, your turtle will never go hungry.

  • Last a long time

    Unlike dogs and cats, western painted turtles can live to be > 50 years old. You and your turtle friend will have a life time of adventures together.
